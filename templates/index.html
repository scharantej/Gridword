Word Search Game

Score:

0

Time Remaining:

60

Instructions:

  1. Drag and drop the letter tiles to form words.
  2. Words must be at least 3 letters long.
  3. Words can be formed in any direction, including diagonally.
  4. Each word you find will score you points.
  5. The game ends when all the words are found or the timer expires.
